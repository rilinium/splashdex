How Frog Rendering Works

Each frog is composited from three layers:
1. Base layer — a grayscale frog silhouette, tinted with the body color.
2. Genus layer — genus-specific markings (spots, stripes, etc.), tinted with the pattern color.
3. Overlay — a shading mask applied with a hard-light blend to add depth and restore eye whites.
Special: Chroma pattern animates through the full RGB spectrum. Glass color makes the frog semi-transparent.
